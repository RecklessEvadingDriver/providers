About Vega Providers

Vega Providers is an open-source collection of streaming content providers designed for the Vega app. Our mission is to provide a fast, reliable, and cross-platform solution for accessing streaming content from various sources around the world.

Each provider is carefully crafted to ensure optimal performance, with support for multiple regions and content types including movies, TV shows, and anime. The providers are built with TypeScript and optimized for minimal bundle size and maximum compatibility.
